Filing Cistem

A short browser game about putting things into boxes (or, more accurately, tubes). You work in a government office, where your job is to sort paperwork into "male" and "female" - all day, every day. Now get to work.

This game was created in collaboration with my friends Coriander, Rat Council, and Kaz Nuckowski, and submitted to the Queer Games Bundle 2022. I did all of the programming (using the Godot game engine), and also came up with the initial concept.

Play on itch.io

Climbing a tall building but you are tied to a hungry seagull

A physics-based game where you must climb to the top of a skyscraper while being dragged in all directions by a bird. Avoid letting the seagull eat any chips and gravy, or it will get stronger!

I made this within 48 hours for the GMTK Game Jam 2021. This was also my first foray into using Godot's RigidBody2D.

"It is a silly game, and I like it" - Mark Brown, Game Maker's Toolkit

Scaling Perpettua

A 2D puzzle game where the floor is collapsing beneath your feet. Watch your step, or you'll fall through the floor onto the previous level.

This demo version was made in four days for the Coding Blocks Game Jam 2021 - my first ever game jam entry!

Memes On This Day (WIP)

An "on this day"-style calendar, with a focus on the history of internet memes. You can look up any day of the year to find an event from meme history which occurred on that day in a past year.

This is currently still a work-in-progress, mostly because more memes are needed to cover all days of the year.
